I’m an illustrator and craftswoman fueled by the joy of creating art that feels real and personal. My practice spans visuals for brands, publications, and people, as well as handcrafted pieces made with natural materials like coconut shells and papier mâché. For me, drawing and making are two sides of the same practice, both rooted in tangible elements that give my work presence, speaking first to the eye, and even more deeply, to touch.
